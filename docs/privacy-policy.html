Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This application ("App") automates posting to Threads using the Threads API. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Access

The App accesses the following data through the Threads API, solely to perform automated posting on behalf of the account owner:

  • Basic Threads account information (user ID, username)
  • Threads post content and engagement metrics (likes, views)

How We Use Information

Accessed information is used exclusively to:

  • Publish posts to the authorized Threads account
  • Retrieve post performance metrics to determine which content to promote

No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose other than operating the App.

Data Storage

Access tokens are stored as encrypted GitHub Actions secrets. Post records are stored in a private GitHub repository accessible only to the account owner. No personal data is stored beyond what is necessary for the App to function.

Data Retention

Post records are retained indefinitely for analytics purposes and may be deleted by the account owner at any time.

Your Rights

You may revoke the App's access to your Threads account at any time through the Threads app settings under "Connected Apps."

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data associated with this App, revoke access through the Threads app under Settings → Account → Apps and websites, then contact us by opening an issue at the project repository. All stored records associated with your account will be deleted within 30 days.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please open an issue at the project repository.
